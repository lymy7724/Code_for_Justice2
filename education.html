Education

Why Education Matters

Education is crucial for migrants for a variety of reasons. One of the main problems that migrants face is discrimination and exclusion in their new host country. This can lead to limited access to job opportunities, social services, and even basic rights. By obtaining an education, migrants can gain important skills and knowledge that can help them navigate and succeed in their new environment. Our mission at Rebel Resources is to connect migrants to those necessary resources.

Resources

ESOL

A challenge that migrants often face is language barriers.Without the ability to communicate effectively in their new language, migrants may struggle to find employment, access healthcare, we work with organizations such as The Immigrant Learning Center which is devoted to helping migrants overcome the language barrier free of charge.

Scholarships for migrant students

Scholarships can be a great resource for migrants to access education and achieve their academic goals. Many organizations like Golden Door Scholars offer scholarships specifically for migrants and refugees, recognizing the unique challenges they face in pursuing higher education.

Colleges for undocumented students

There are several colleges and universities in the United States that openly welcome undocumented students and provide resources to support their academic success
